ABOUT

Welcome to Toyyibah Associates Limited

Toyyibah Associates Limited provides a hands-on approach to managing projects from inception to completion. By deploying customized resources to meet the demands of each client supported by strong technical resources, our professionals provide an end-to- end solution that discerning companies have come to depend on for different sizes of projects.

Our People

Our vision, our total dedication to clients’ services and our resources enable us to identify and recruit best technical professionals in all aspects of our services. Our timely and Clients’ satisfaction policies are anchored on a team of well-trained, experienced and honest professionals.

Our Mission

Toyyibah is committed to driving positive change in Project Management, Design and Build, Construction Management, Facility/Maintenance Management business by using best-of-breed technology, professionalism, unparalleled clients’ satisfaction and with a firm belief in the principle of excellence.

Our Vision

To be one of the preferred indigenous Project Management, Design and Build, Construction Management, Facility/Maintenance Management firms in the country achieved by uncommon clients’ satisfaction.

Our Value

Client-focused, integrity and dedication all built on strong ethical foundation, responsiveness and uncompromising quality.

Quality Control Statement

Our quality control policy is to clearly identify and meet the quality requirements of our Clients and to actively and continuously promotes the achievement of excellence by ;

1. Implementation of quality systems and procedures of international standards
2. Optimum compliance with all contractual requirements
3. To continuously evaluate our quality system and procedures through periodic internal quality audit and seeking further improvements.

Promise

Each Client is guaranteed to have total satisfaction through;
1. High quality project that is professionally and technologically handled
2. Timely completion without unnecessary delay
3. Reasonable and affordable contract sum
4. Superior Clients’ relations services which place the Clients rightly as our basis of our continued existence

Health and Safety Policy

Our health and safety policy is anchored on provision of safest and healthiest working conditions and environment by adopting health and safety measures of local and international standards.
